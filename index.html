Mission

There are a lot of eLearning companies starting out right now. What makes us different is that we are not about selling content. Most companies fragment the educational world further. We want to unite people on their road to a better education and empower them to help each other out, creating content for each other.
We want to open up the priced possessions of the world's best schools, much like Aristotle did thousands of years ago. We are not building paywalls, we are tearing them down. Because basic education should be free but also getting higher in quality.
We want to create a world in which teachers can focus on answering questions instead of repeating the same talk they've repeated over the last 20 years. A world in which students can prepare more efficiently and better paced than ever before.



Pricing

Good news! Aristotle is free because we want to bring education to the entire world. There might be a time when we allow for teachers to earn money by providing content. But for now, it's free contribution and free usage.
